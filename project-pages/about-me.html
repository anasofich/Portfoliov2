About me :)

Hi! My name is Ana Sofia Castellanos and I am a Digital Designer based in Copenhagen. From a young age, I have always had an eye for aesthetics and passion for design, fashion, visual arts, and technology.

I moved to Copenhagen in the summer of 2020 to study the Multimedia Design AP at Copenhagen School of Design and Technology, KEA. During my time at KEA, I discovered my great passion for media, digital and web design. I love the way that digital design allows me to bring my creations to life in a way that is interactive and engaging. I get inspired in my designs by the world around me, so growing up in Mexico really influenced many of my works to be very colorful as you might notice:)

I am constantly seeking out new inspiration, trends, and experimenting with design techniques. If you have a great idea in mind, reach out!

Besides design, I also like music, going to concerts, hitting the dancefloor, outdoor activities, hiking, trying new cuisines, traveling, learning about new cultures and languages, etc etc.
