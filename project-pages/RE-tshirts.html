RE: sustainable t-shirts

Tools used: Xd, Ps, Ai, Lr
RE: sustainable T-shirts, was a group project during the first semester of my education. For this project, we had to create an XD prototype of a mobile solution of a small t-shirt webshop with a defined target audience. The purpose was to experience a selection of UX methods and to learn how to present a product and communicate our research and test results to stakeholders

scroll scroll scroll scroll scroll

from testing...

We started off the project by sketching many ideas and creating crazy 8s, in order to determine which type of webshop we would create. We ended up deciding to create a webshop of sustainable t-shirts made out of recycled materials targeting an audience of people 20-30.

After having a better idea of how the web-shop would look like, we created a UX storyboard and a paper prototype to test our solution.

... to prototyping

Once we had tested our paper prototype, we gathered the main findings in order to make a list of improvements.

We then applied these to the final prototype on Adobe XD, where we had to create the entire purchase flow of a webshop focusing on delivering a good UX and UI.

the solution

When the first version of the final solution was finished, we ran several Think-Aloud Tests, which gave us a great insight into what things were not working in the design since they made the UX complicated because some users did not understand some features.

We applied the suggested improvements to the solution, creating a final proof-of-concept solution that received very positive feedback.
