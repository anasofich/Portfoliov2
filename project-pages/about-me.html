About me :)

Hi! My name is Ana Sofia Castellanos, I am 23 years old and I am Mexican and Swedish, but I've lived most of my life in Guadalajara, Mexico. I moved to Copenhagen in the summer of 2020 to start studying the Multimedia Design AP at Copenhagen School of Design and Technology, KEA. During my time at KEA, I have discovered my great passion for media, digital and web design. I like to create user friendly solutions with an attractive and functional design.

Since I was younger, I've had a big passion for design, fashion, visual arts and technology. That is why I decided to study this program. I get inspired in my designs by the world around me, so growing up in Mexico really influenced many of my works to be very colorful as you might notice:)

Besides design, I also like music, going to concerts, outdoor activities, hiking, trying new cuisines, traveling, learning about new cultures and languages, etc etc.
