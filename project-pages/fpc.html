film photography club

Tools used: Figma, HTML, CSS, JS, rest.db, Ps, Ai
Film Photography Club was a school project in which together with a group, we had to find a common interest and create a website related to it, which could be one giving general information about the topic or creating a community. An important requirement was to implement a dynamic feature in the site that would fetch and post data from/to a database.

scroll scroll scroll scroll scroll

Finding a common interest

First of all, we had to create groups based on an interest we had in common. I joined a group with two other girls and our common interest was film photography

We decided to create a website that would not only introduce newbies to film photography, but it would also be a community in which people share tips about film photography, share their photographs on the portfolio page, and it even includes a list of different film cameras where you can filter the cameras depending on what you‚Äôre looking for.

Designing the website

After having an idea of what we wanted to include on the website, we defined a target audience and made personas. With the help of the personas, we made style tiles and started deciding on the design of the website that would be suitable for the target audience.

We also did some research on some of the most popular film cameras based on type, level and format, and created a database of these cameras to create the ‚Äúfind your match‚Äù page that fetches data from rest.db by using an API to display the cameras on the site.

The solution

At the end we created a fully functional site with a lot of different and relevant features for both newbies to advanced photographers.
